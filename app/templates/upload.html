Supported: Chase, Bank of America, Capital One, Wells Fargo, Amex, Generic
Credit card charges will be displayed as -$ (money out), refunds as +$ (green), and card payments as transfers (yellow, excluded from Schedule C).
How It Works
  1. Select one or more CSV files exported from your bank(s).
  2. The app auto-detects the bank format and parses every transaction.
  3. Each transaction is sent to qwen2.5:14b running locally — your data never leaves this machine.
  4. The AI assigns a Schedule C category with a confidence level.
  5. You review and approve (or correct) the categories on the Transactions page.
  6. The Reports page shows your final Schedule C totals, ready for your CPA.
Meals are automatically flagged — the 50% deductibility cap is applied in Reports.
Duplicates are detected by date + description + amount and skipped.
